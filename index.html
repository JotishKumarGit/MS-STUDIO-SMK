Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of furniture do you offer? +
We offer handcrafted wooden furniture including sofas, beds, dining sets, office furniture, and custom-designed pieces.
2. Can I order customized furniture? +
Yes! We provide fully customizable furniture solutions tailored to your space, style, and material preferences.
3. What materials do you use for your furniture? +
We use premium solid wood, eco-friendly finishes, and high-quality hardware to ensure durability and elegance.
4. How long does delivery take? +
Delivery typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on the product and customization. We provide estimated delivery time at checkout.
5. Do you provide installation services? +
Yes, professional installation is available for larger items like beds, wardrobes, and dining sets.
6. What is your return and warranty policy? +
We offer a 7-day return policy for damaged or defective items and a 1-year warranty on all furniture.
7. Are your products eco-friendly? +
Yes! We use sustainable wood and eco-friendly finishes to reduce environmental impact.
8. Can I track my order? +
Yes, we provide tracking details via email and SMS once your order is shipped.
9. Do you offer bulk or corporate orders? +
Yes, we provide special pricing and customized solutions for bulk orders and corporate clients.
10. How can I care for my wooden furniture? +
Clean with a soft, dry cloth and avoid direct sunlight or water exposure. For deeper cleaning, use furniture polish recommended for wood.
